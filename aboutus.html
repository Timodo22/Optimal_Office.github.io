Our founders

Optimal Office was founded by Lucas Gielens and Martijn van Oers, driven by a shared passion for creating inspiring work environments. We believe that a well-designed workspace can transform productivity and enhance how people feel at work.

Elevate Your Workspace with Optimal Office
